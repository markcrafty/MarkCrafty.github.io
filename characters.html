Bio

Alacard is unlike the rest of his kind. They are ruthless killers, wanting nothing but to siphon power.
He was made an offer to join the Void Peddlers, a group of deserters like him, but declined.
He instead went around the world, meeting new people and visiting new locations.
In Nelvia, he made a bond with a girl named Toka, who shared his love of advanced weaponry.
After a while, it became a habit for him to check in on her, as she had no family of her own.

All was well, until the day Paraxim assaulted the city.

His forces took the city, destroying everything outside the main walls.
Alacard returned during the siege. In a blind rage, he slaughtered Paraxim's forces and his advisor, Kazhix.
Toka's survival may be uncertain, but one thing is: Alacard will not stop hunting Paraxim until he knows what happened to her.

Skills

Weapon: Twin Pistols

Wildfire Grenade:
Deal 4x Strength damage (Elements: Fire) to an enemy. If the target is above 70% health, apply Burn and deal 100% more damage. If the target is already affected by Burn, Critically Strike.

Rapid Fire:
Deal 2x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy 5 times. You can select all enemies, causing the damage to be split evenly among them.

Master Huntsman:
Passive: Alacard's attacks can bypass Taunt. Gunmaster: Switch between Dual Shot and Quickshot at will (Dual Shot damages 2 different enemies, Quickshot deals higher damage to 1).

Interdiction Rifle:
Heroic: Alacard brings out his most powerful gun, the Interdiction Rifle. It can shoot 3 specialized bolts before overheating. These bolts deal 6x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) and ignore Armor.

Trivia

Alacard released in Patch 0.2.0: Edge of Reality, alongside the dungeon by the same name.

Alacard's name is very similar to Alucard, who I didn't know existed during his creation.

Alacard had a fanclub in 0.3.2, all the way up to 0.7.0.

Bio

Vynfall was once Camellia's home, back when it was known as Forest's Edge.
Vyn's original defeat came with a curse: The area was diseased and darkened, and his soul was forever locked beneath the earth.
All who lived there had left to find new homes, but during a survey of the damage, a mage deserted her party to try and harness Vyn's soul. Her attempts failed, but she would always try again.
Many years later, Camellia returned to her past home to see if she could find any of her family's belongings. During this time, she found the mage. But she was too late to stop her.
Vyn suddenly sprung to life
His rise came with the mage fleeing, and him wounding Camellia with a shadowy scythe just before she escaped.

While Vyn had returned, he was tied to his resting place, only able to be a commander to his master's armies.
Now, Camellia waits in Ephilar for adventurers brave enough to help her take down Vyn once and for all.

Skills

Weapon: Bayonet

Close Combat:
Deal 2x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy twice, then lower their Physical Armor by 50% (For 2 turns) and strike again.

Blade Dance:
Deal 3x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) to 2 different enemies. If any are Stunned, Critically Strike.

Perfect Parry:
Passive: Camellia has a 10% chance to perform Perfect Parry (Deal 4x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) and apply Stun for 2 turns) after taking Physical damage.

Tiny Camellia:
Heroic: Camellia summons a tiny version of herself to deal 4x Strength damage (Elements: Physical, Fire) to all enemies. She then applies Bleed to them.

Trivia

Camellia released in Patch 0.8.0: Leylight Hollow, alongside Rayzon and the Jelly Overgrowth's redesigns.

Camellia was planned for about a month before finally releasing due to no work being done on her face sprite.

Camellia was created entirely by Xelynaria and FanStalin.

Bio

Neraz was once one of the 4 Generals of Voragon's army.
He was a masterful swordsman, and had fought in many wars before his life was claimed by Paraxim.
After years of being controlled, Voragon's grip began to weaken. He plotted in secret to escape his dark masters.
When the time was right, he made his move. He caused a storm of frost and blood to crash upon the citadel.
But, Neraz was not the only one who fled.
Many of Voragon's minions deserted alongside him, even another general: Zivian.
Voragon, in a furious rage, sent Nocturnus after Zivian, while he had Paraxim hunt down Neraz and the deserters.
Neraz eventually arrived in a small forest town called Nelvia, and stayed there for 3 years.
As time passed, the city grew, and his faction had become allies of the Nekim.

Neraz has been awaiting the day Paraxim finds him.
He was eager to kill a general.

Skills

Weapon: Sword

Northern Blade:
Neraz's blade freezes over, causing his next 3 Basic Attacks to deal +20% damage and decrease the target's Speed by 20% for 3 turns. (If the target is Bleeding, gain Taste of Blood, increase this skill's bonus by 20%, and refresh the remaining charges.)

Frozen Strike:
Deals 4x Strength damage (Elements: Frost) to an enemy and apply Slowness to them. (Also apply Deep Wounds if Neraz has Taste of Blood.)

Lich's Shield:
Passive: Neraz's Basic Attacks generate a Shield equal to the damage they deal, but cost 5% of his Max HP. (The target pays the price if Neraz has Taste of Blood.)

Life Conduit:
Heroic: Neraz infuses a conduit of blood into an ally, causing their next 3 attacks to deal 2x Neraz's Strength damage to their target, and heal for the same amount. (Another random ally gains the conduit if Neraz has Taste of Blood.)

Trivia

Neraz released in the very first patch, making him one of the 8 oldest characters.

Neraz replaced a character codenamed "Quickblade", and was originally designed on a car ride.

Neraz used to summon undead, but was redesigned due to the system being buggy.

Bio

Sel, unlike the rest of her people, disliked conflict with the other humanoid races of Ephilon.
She much prefered joining the Void Peddlers, a neutral faction of Void Elves, who exist to make a profit.
During her time working with them, she accepted missions to gather supplies and kill deadly creatures.
On one such mission, she came accross the hut of an alchemist named Daxt, who decided to show her some of his Toxins.
They eventually decided to strike a deal: Daxt supplies Sel with Toxins, and she would deliver him gold each week.

Sel, with her newfound toxic weapons, returned to her original mission.
Her and Daxt kept up their trade for weeks, until Xelni decided to teach Sel about making them herself.
Relations between the Peddlers and Daxt were damaged when he learned about this, but he didn't hold his grudges for long.

Now, Sel continues her missions. But, a curious adventurer arrived recently...Hyria. And Sel wants to get to know her.

Skills

Weapon: Daggers

Sadistic Nature:
Deal 4x Strength damage (Elements: Physical, Shadow) to an enemy. If they're inflicted with a Toxin, deal 7x Strength damage instead.

Trail of Shadows:
For 3 turns, Sel has a base 40% (Increased based on 5% of her luck) chance to dodge enemy attacks. Upon a successful dodge, the attacker is inflicted with \c[3]\i[46]Crippling Toxin\c[0] and another random one.

Envenom Weapon:
Passive: Sel's Basic Attacks have a base 40% (Increased based on 5% of her luck) chance to apply a Toxin to enemies. When she applies a Toxin, she heals for 5% of her Max HP.

Intoxicating Finisher:
Heroic: Deal 4x Strength damage (Elements: Physical, Shadow) to an enemy. Deal +2x Strength damage for each Toxin applied this battle.

Trivia

Sel was one of the 4 original Legends, making her one of the 8 oldest characters.

Sel and Gailan used to hate each other in lore. Then they became friends, and now neither know the other exists.

Sel's old Heroic, Assassination, could deal the most damage out of any skill in a single hit, ranking at 96x Strength damage (Not including outside damage boosts)

Bio

Jade always looked up to those who defended her home city, Skyfold.
So, it came as no surprise that she began training to become like them.
Eventually, Jade was the first female guard of the city, and protected it like it was her own child.

But then, Magmardon errupted.
The slumbering mountain had grown volatile from the fire elementals living within, and now they were unleashed on Desolin and it's cities.
Jade was there to help evacuate Skyfold, and even wounded a general of flame.

While not everyone escaped, she did what she could, and hoped the others could make it out too...



...Jade's story continues. The city of Eldan was home to many plagues and curses, one of which was Sanguara.
Jade, and 3 others, used the amulets they wielded to seal Sanguara beneath the city.
They built 4 additional seals around Ephilon to ensure she could not escape...but her influence has spread to a major trade company known as Visionary.

Skills

Weapon: Spear and Shield

Shared Between Stances:

Armory:
Switch between Keeper and Avenger Stance at will (Keeper is a Tank, Avenger is an Assassin).

Push on!:
Heroic: Increases allies' Speed by 10% and Crit Rate by 4% until you exit the dungeon. This effect can stack up to 3 times.

Keeper Stance:

Sentry:
Select an ally. Jade takes all damage in their place. 50% of damage the chosen ally deals is restored as health to Jade. (Recast this on the target, or another target, to remove the effect).

Bring it!:
Deal 4x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy. Their next attack will be redirected to Jade. After that attack finishes, Jade will deal 4x Strength damage and Stun them.

Tough Skin:
Passive: After Jade takes damage, she gains a Shield against that element, reducing future damage by 20% for 3 turns.

Avenger Stance:

Heartseeker:
Deal 2x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy. Crit damage is increased by 4x Critical Rate. Damage is increased by 100% if the target is below 20% health. Always Critically Strikes.

Avenging Strike:
Deal 3x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy. Damage is increased by 1% for every 5 health the enemy has more than Jade, up to a maximum of 300%. This ignores 50% of the target's Armor.

Perseverance:
Passive: While below 50% health, Jade receives 50% more healing. While below 25%, she receives 100% more healing. This effect does not work on indirect sources.

Trivia

Jade released in Patch 0.10.0: Eldan's Fate, which featured Sel and Elderon's redesigns.

Jade sparked an idea for a storyline involving 4 different types of stones.

Jade was created entirely by Maik. This is his 2nd original character added to Ephilar.

Bio

Lily is a host for infernal things.
She struck a deal with Jin'liq to save her life from Magmardon's assualt: Her soul, for power.
During combat within the caverns of Mag'mar, Jin will take control to save his host from death.

Once Lily had razed the caverns and slain Forgebane, Magmardon's general, she went off to find more evil to slay.
Jin always remained vigilant, making sure Lily would never fall. He was bound via contract, after all.

Now she arrives in Farlon: A safe haven. She wishes to take measures to rid herself of Jin.
But for now, they are eternally bound.

Skills

Weapon: Chain

Firewall:
Lily creates a massive wall of flames for 3 turns, causing enemy Ranged attacks to be destroyed, and ally Ranged attacks to inflict Burn.

Deflagrate:
Deal damage (Elements: Fire) to all enemies equal to 10% of Lily's current Health 3 times. Costs 10% of her current Health.

Flame Barrier:
For 1 turn, gain a Shield equal to 15% of Lily's Max HP for each enemy, and absorb all damage taken by allies. When damage is absorbed, deal 4x Spell Damage (Elements: Fire) to the attacker.

Burning Pact:
Passive: Whenever Lily loses 15% of her Health from an attack, she gains a stack of Burning Pact, increasing her stats by 5% for each stack. At 5 stacks, she gains Hellfire Pact (Attack twice a turn, 35% chance to inflict Burn, uncontrollable).

Bio

Honor. Justice. These are the things Mitch knows best.
Ever since he lost the man he called brother, however, he's been out for revenge.
Nocturnus tore him apart on that terrible day. The town him and Mitch had spent so long building, destroyed.

Mitch has been honing his techniques, awaiting the day Nocturnus returns.
During this troublesome time, he met a bright and young girl named Phoenix.
They bonded over their losses, but Mitch couldn't shake the feeling that they had met before.

Destiny called again, and a Void General came to hunt Mitch. It wasn't Nocturnus, but he still took pride in his kill.
Darkhan was dead, and the chances of Voragon getting another darkmage were slim.

Mitch and Phoenix parted ways afterwards, feeling they should journey alone.

Skills

Weapon: Axe

Swift Chop:
Deal 3x Strength Damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy and inflict them with Deep Wounds. Adrenaline: This skill is Unstoppable (Cannot miss, be redirected, or have it's damage reduced).

Keen Chop:
Deal 3x Physical Damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy. If the target is afflicted with Deep Wounds, apply Bleed. Adrenaline: Critically Strike.

Zealous Sweep:
Deal 4x Physical Damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy. If the target is affected with Deep Wounds, reduce this skill's cooldown by 1 turn. Adrenaline: Refund 75% of the Mana used to cast this.

Victorious Sweep:
Deal 5x Physical Damage to an enemy. Heal for 3x Intellect Damage if they have less health than you. Adrenaline: Heal is based off Strength and occurs regardless.

Defy Pain:
Mitch gains +20% Max Health and takes -10 damage from all sources for 2 turns. Adrenaline: Gain Taunt and increase the damage reduction to -30. This can't be used while Battle Rage is active.

Battle Rage:
Mitch can take an additonal action each turn, but deals 25% less damage for 2 turns. Adrenaline: Your skills refund 50% of Adrenaline used. This can't be used while Defy Pain is active.

Thrill of Battle:
Passive: Mitch's basic attacks grant him 20 Adrenaline, and gains 1 Adrenaline per 3 HP lost. If available, Mitch will consume 40 Adrenaline to empower his skills.

Trivia

Lily released in the very first patch, making her one of the 8 oldest characters.

Alacard was originally going to be in the base game, but Lily took his place.

Lily was created entirely by FanStalin. She was later redesigned by Maik.
