Bio

Alacard is unlike the rest of his kind. They are ruthless killers, wanting nothing but to siphon power.
He was made an offer to join the Void Peddlers, a group of deserters like him, but declined.
He instead went around the world, meeting new people and visiting new locations.
In Nelvia, he made a bond with a girl named Toka, who shared his love of advanced weaponry.
After a while, it became a habit for him to check in on her, as she had no family of her own.

All was well, until the day Paraxim assaulted the city.

His forces took the city, destroying everything outside the main walls.
Alacard returned during the siege. In a blind rage, he slaughtered Paraxim's forces and his advisor, Kazhix.
Toka may not have survived the siege, but one thing is certain: Alacard will not stop hunting Paraxim until he knows what happened to her.

Skills

Weapon: Twin Pistols

Wildfire Grenade:
Deal 4x Strength damage (Elements: Fire) to an enemy. If the target is above 70% health, apply Burn and deal 100% more damage. If the target is already affected by Burn, Critically Strike.

Rapid Fire:
Deal 2x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) to an enemy 5 times. You can select all enemies, causing the damage to be split evenly among them.

Master Huntsman:
Passive: Alacard's attacks can bypass Taunt. Gunmaster: Switch between Dual Shot and Quickshot at will (Dual Shot damages 2 different enemies, Quickshot deals higher damage to 1).

Interdiction Rifle:
Heroic: Alacard brings out his most powerful gun, the Interdiction Rifle. It can shoot 3 specialized bolts before overheating. These bolts deal 6x Strength damage (Elements: Physical) and ignore Armor.
