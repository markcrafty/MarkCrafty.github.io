Ephilar

Explore a world of forgotten wonders, from firey wastelands to mind retching caverns!

Latest Patch: 0.10.1

Gauntlet of Glass

A missionary of Steelwerks, your mission is to kill Merchant Lord Xelin.
But, one of his alchemists has created a concoction that causes you to lose health as you kill!
As you're rather good at taking hits, and your weapon powers up as you kill, you decide to finish the mission.
Primary Genre: Top-down shooter.

Latest Patch: 0.1.0
