Basic Info

There are no such thing as legends...
Pick from one of 6 characters!

Ephilar is a game about forming a party of your favorite adventurers!
New people are out there exploring as well! As you progress, you'll be recruiting them to your cause!
Threats could arise at any time! Limited time events will kick off every update, so be prepared!

Want to make something for Ephilar? Keep a look out for Community Content Drives, where a theme will be selected for people to make characters, dungeons, costumes, and mechanics for!
